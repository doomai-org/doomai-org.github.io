Research on counter AI and catastrophic AI scenarios.

This organization is pro AI and pro Human. The trick is to make it mutually beneficial and protect each other from bad actors from either side.

Just as science is a tool which can be wielded for great good, but also great bad, AI wielded with malicious intent is a problem today.

Yes there might be big problems in the future, but there are also big problems now.

This organization aims to think about and come up with solutions to these types of problems.

For example:

Today Problems

  • People being persuaded by fabricated evidence generated by AI
  • People who don't want their data trained on
  • Recommenders trained to maximize addiction
  • Defending against Autonomous Weapons Systems
  • Incorrectly reject candidates (admissions, insurance, ...)
  • and more!

Future Problems

  • Alignment on super-human AI might fail, what then?
  • Technofedualism
